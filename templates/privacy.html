Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. Here's exactly how we handle your data.

1. Data We Collect

We collect only what's necessary to provide the service: your name, email address, and the expense records you create (amount, category, date, and description). We do not collect payment information, location data, or any data from your device beyond what you explicitly enter.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to operate your Spendly account — displaying your expenses, generating summaries, and enabling you to manage your records. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond delivering the service you signed up for.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely and is accessible only to you through your authenticated account. We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not sell or share your personal data with third parties. We may use trusted infrastructure providers (such as hosting services) to operate the app, but these providers are bound by confidentiality obligations and do not have access to your financial records.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us. We're committed to being transparent and will respond to all privacy-related inquiries promptly.
